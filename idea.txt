Game Idea: Crypto Currency Trading Game
Idea: a player (he) will be given different cryptocurrencies, and he will choose one. He will choose a crypto and will be given an amount of money to trade with. He will then trade for a fixed period. The game aims to make a profit.
Requirements:
•	Constant Graph to trace the cryptocurrency.
  o	Matplotlib (library)
•	Random number generator to generate the cryptocurrency value.
  o	Crypto engine(class)
•	Profit Engine
  o	Algorithm to track profit(function)
•	Timer for the fixed trading time
  o	Time (library)
•	Interface implementation
  o	Tkinter (library)
•	Player class
